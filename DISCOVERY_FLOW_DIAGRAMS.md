## ðŸ§¬ Technical Deep Dive

### What is MulticastLock?

```kotlin
val wifiManager = getSystemService(WIFI_SERVICE) as WifiManager
val multicastLock = wifiManager.createMulticastLock("ZapShare:MulticastLock")
multicastLock.acquire()  // â† This enables multicast reception!
```

**What it does:**
1. Tells Android "I need to receive multicast packets"
2. Android configures WiFi chip to accept multicast traffic
3. Multicast packets are no longer filtered out
4. Your app can now discover other devices

**Battery Trade-off:**
- Without lock: Better battery (0% overhead)
- With lock: Minimal battery cost (~1% per hour)
- Worth it for discovery functionality!
