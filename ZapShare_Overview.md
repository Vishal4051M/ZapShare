## âš¡ How to Connect

ZapShare offers versatile ways to connect your devices:

1.  **Auto-Discovery Radar:**
    *   Open the app on two devices on the same Wi-Fi.
    *   They "see" each other on the radar instantly.
    *   Tap the device icon to request a connection.

2.  **Magic Code:**
    *   Sender gets a unique **8-character code** (e.g., `12X45Y78`).
    *   Receiver enters this code to verify and connect instantly.
    *   Perfect for busy networks where auto-discovery might be crowded.

3.  **QR Code:**
    *   Sender displays a QR code.
    *   Receiver scans it to link up immediately.
